About Our Research Process

Reviews & Grading

Our research team conducts thousands of phone interviews
each year with homeowners. A company much achieve an
overall A grade from more than 100 homeowner reviews to be
eligible for Best Pick certification.

License & Insurance Verification

We contact the relevant government bodies to verify
that each company holds all state-required trade licenses.
We also contact each company’s insurance providers to confirm
that they have general liability insurance and any additional
state-required insurance, including workers’ compensation.

We are certificate holders for each Best Pick company,
meaning we hold a certified copy of their insurance policies.

Homeowner-Backed Recommendations

By interviewing homeowners directly over the phone
and excluding opt-in reviews and references, we make sure that
the feedback we collect is not from a biased vocal minority, as
is commonly the case with online review sites.

Inclusion in our report is by invitation alone. Companies
cannot buy their way in or pay for increased exposure on
our website.
